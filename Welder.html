Miller 325 Gas Welder

Professional Gas-Powered Welder

Description

The Miller 325 Gas Welder is a powerful, portable welding machine designed for professional fabrication and heavy-duty welding applications. With an impressive 12,000 watts of power generation, this gas-powered unit delivers reliable welding performance anywhere on the job site without the need for external electricity.

Equipped with approximately 70 feet of welding cable, this welder provides excellent reach and flexibility for large-scale projects. Whether you're working on structural steel, pipeline fabrication, farm equipment repair, or construction projects, the Miller 325 delivers consistent arc quality and exceptional welding results.
